Badge Collector


A number will be randomly generated at the start of the game. Your goal is to match this number by clicking on the badges below. Each badge will have a randomly generated value at the start of the game. Clicking the badges will add to your score.

You WIN when total value of your badges match the number.
You LOSE when the total value of your badges go over the number
